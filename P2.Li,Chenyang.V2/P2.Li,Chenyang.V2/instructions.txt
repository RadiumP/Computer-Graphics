Right-button menu:

a. Image: 1 uphill: change the background pic to  'hill'
	  2 ground: change the pic to 'flat ground'
b. StickManMove: Default: the stickman will be doing exercises. If you left-click the screen, it will generate new one. 
			The screen will only contain 3 stickmen at most. The stickmen will move in both 'uphill' and 'ground'.
		 Walking: only one stickman will walk in the index card. If in 'ground', the stickman will go back and forth on the ground.
			 If in 'uphill', the stickman will climb up and down.
c. Filled: On: the index card will be filled in blue
	   Off: the color will be turned off.
