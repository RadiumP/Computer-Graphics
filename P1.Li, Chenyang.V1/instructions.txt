1.To flip picture.
  In order to demonstrate that the program can flip the background picture.
  I left the starting picture with the original one. To flip the picture,  
  follow the steps.
  a.Use right click to display the menu.
  b.Use the submenu 'Image'to select '1.png'.
  c.The picture flips.



2.To draw things.
    a.Use right click to display the menu.
    b.Use the submenu 'Draw' to select 'Circle','Square' or 'Line' to display
    either circle or square or line.
    c.The shape is drawn in the index card.
    PS:The objects will look better if you flip the '1.png' first.

3.To get RGB
    You can left-click on the screen to show the (x,y) and the RGB.
    Using the test.png to test the click is recommended.
    a.Use right click to display the menu.
    b.Use the submenu 'Image'to select 'test.png'.
    c.Left-click the screen.
    d.Get the info from the console.
